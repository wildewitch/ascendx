The Auto-Chef from AscendX is here to impress.



No time to cook? That's okay with us. We still think you deserve a five-star meal. Meet the AscendX Auto-Chef, created by foodies, for foodies.


Simply enter the recipe for the meal you want into the Auto-Chef's interface and watch it go. The Auto-Chef will create your meal from scratch to your exact specifications using the ingredients you supply it with yourself, using the included Auto-Chef Pantry.


Whether it's a romantic dinner, a luxurious dessert, a quick lunch for the kids or even a simple salad, your kitchen is in good hands.

Onward and Upward


AscendX Future Corp is a diversified technologies company and government contractor, dedicated to empowering the people of today with the technologies of tomorrow. It was founded as Ascent Industries in 1949 before its acquisition and rebranding by the Wancheng Global Interest Group and its subsidiaries in 2024.

AscendX is composed of two branches: Military Applications Research & Development (MARD), and Consumer Relations & Quality of Life (CRQL). The company is best known for its creation of the AX-RG Nautical Railgun and the AX Peacekeeper Corps, the world's first autonomous robotic police force.

AscendX is proudly founded in the United States of America and headquartered in Chicago, Illinois. In addition to its US headquarters, AscendX has sister divisions in the United Kingdom, South Korea and China, making up the powerhouse Wancheng Global x AscendX conglomerate known as Bermuda International.
